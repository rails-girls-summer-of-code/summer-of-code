The Rails Girls Summer of Code could not be possible without the wonderful support of the community members.

  • Become a coach: You are a developer and want to support a team of students? Find all the information about working side-by-side with students on their project.

  • Become a Coaching Company: Your company is passionate about Open Source and has space / time to spare for a team? Find out how to become a Coaching Company.

  • Become a Project Mentor: You are core contributor to a great Open Source project and want to give students the opportunity to work on it? Read all about what it means to mentor an RGSoC team.

  • Become a Supervisor: Are you keen on directly supporting 1-2 teams with their organizational and non-coding issues and provide moral support? Check what it takes to become a supervisor.

  • Become a Sponsor: You want to support our mission of getting more women working on Open Source projects? Find out how all about our sponsor packages and perks.
